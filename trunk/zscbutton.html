Share Counters and Buttons

zSCbutton is an all-in-one share counters / share buttons widget for blogs/websites. It includes most popular OFFICIAL share buttons including Facebook Like button, Twitter Tweet button, Google Plus One button, and in addition a Lintas Berita button.

This is not a third party button/widget, it only combines OFFICIAL codes from those social networking sites into one good-looking widget.

Go to the widget homepage for a complete step-by-step guide on how to put this on your blogs or websites and some more information.

The widget is available in three forms/shapes: Horizontal Wide, Horizontal Tall, and Vertical widget. Put one of these codes into your blogs/webpages template/theme:
